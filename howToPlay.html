This is a 2-player split-screen soccer game.
  • W A S D or on-screen HUD to control the red robot.
  • J K L I or on-screen HUD to control the blue robot.
Camera angle and zoom can be controlled with the mouse. The first player to get the ball into the enemy's goal 5 times wins the game.
